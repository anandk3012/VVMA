About VVMA

Collection of performances on various sabhas, stages and concerts. Lessons and techniques by Smt.Shanthi Kurella and other well-known gurus on the subject of Carnatic Classical VOCAL, VEENA and other major instruments. Seasonal competitions to encourage students and followers. A stage to celebrate vaggeyakara jayanthi utsavams, vardhanthis and other Major Hindu festivals. A stage to present Classical Music talent for all levels of Artists.
